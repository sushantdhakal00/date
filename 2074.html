NEPALI CALENDAR 2074

Nepali Calendar 2074 known as Bikram sambat is a collection of calendars with english dates (A.D) also called gregorian calendar with nepali calendar. Nepali calendar includes all the events and festivals of Nepal with detail accuracy.
