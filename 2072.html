NEPALI CALENDAR 2072

Nepali Calendar 2072 known as Bikram sambat is a collection of calendars with english dates (A.D) also called gregorian calendar with nepali calendar. Nepali calendar includes all the events and festivals of Nepal with detail accuracy.

Nepali Patro 2072 with each month having a special religious ritual assigned to it like Dashain, Tihar, Buddha jayanti, Holi, Marriage/wedding dates and many others with tithi's. This year - Dashain 2076 falls on month of Ashwin.
